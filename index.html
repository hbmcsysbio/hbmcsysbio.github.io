Transcriptional Systems Biology Group

To decode the coding genome, many genes controlling critical agronomic features, such as growth, yields, stress resistance, have been identified.

To decoded the non-coding genome, we are making efforts to study transcriptional control --- how genes are switched on and off in a well coordinated manner.

To this end, we combine high-throughtput technologies and computational analysis, to develop and apply sequencing-based methods for plant science.
